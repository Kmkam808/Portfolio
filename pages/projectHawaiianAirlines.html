Date:

Software Used:

Adobe Photoshop

Description:

I made this project in my Art 112 Intro to Digital Art course. I was tasked to make an ad for Hawaiian Airlines. I had to utilize the Hawaiian Airlines logo and the phrase "Hawaii flies with us." I decided to do a split of Hawaii and Japan because at the time I really wanted to visit Japan. So, I took the two well-known things known to Hawaii and Japan and created one cohesive image through taking one half of each mountain and tree. The Japan side (left side) has Mount Fuji and a cherry blossom tree. The Hawaii side (right side) has Diamond Head and a plumeria flower tree.
